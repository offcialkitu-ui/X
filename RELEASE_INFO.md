# What’s New in Melody X

### ✨ Smoother Visuals (Beta)
* **Liquid Glass Enhancements:** Enjoy a more consistent look with adaptive text colors for the miniplayer and navigation bar that match your theme perfectly.
* **Polished Interface:** We’ve decluttered the Settings page, removing redundant cards for a cleaner, more intuitive experience.
* **UI Fixes:** Resolved scrolling issues in the Liquid Glass settings for a seamless navigation experience.

### 🎶 Next-Level Playback (Beta)
* **AutoMix Transitions:** Experience DJ-style, beat-matched crossfades for professional-sounding transitions between tracks (Special thanks to @barathsuresh).
* **Performance Boost:** Optimized our beat-analysis engine to prevent audio stuttering and UI lag, even on mid-range devices.
* **Music Extraction:** Seamlessly integrated the new Echo Extractor, which now automatically stays up-to-date in the background every 24 hours.

### 🛡️ Stability & Performance
* **Crash Fix:** Resolved a critical issue that caused the app to crash during library updates for some users (Fixed by @jester-sys).
* **Under-the-Hood Hardening:** Improved background fetching and memory management to ensure the app stays fast and responsive during long listening sessions.
* **Lossless Progress:** Track our progress toward Qobuz lossless integration directly in Player Settings.

### 🌐 Global & Community
* **Hola!** We’ve reached 100% translation coverage for Spanish, including all-new features like Echo Brain and Spotify Import (Powered by @weblate).
